FAQ's

Is ExMyB an investor itself?
No, Presently ExMyB only helps to connect business (startups/SMEs etc.) with investors.
What is this BAT all about? What is the special part about it?
BAT is arguably your best way to connect with investors and possible fast forward the procedure (subject to qualification). Also, through BAT, ExMyB would help you to under the gap analysis if any.
How to register as an Investor?
Create your investor profile through the homepage and look at potential business ideas or fellow investors.
Does ExMyB reveal my personal details?
Information around your interest basis investment / business is put across in an intelligent manner for the other person to have an understanding of your proposition. Post that, if deals progresses forward, ExMyB holds the right to let the parties know about other details too post validation.
Post successful connection; are there any formalities that needs to be completed?
Yes, once the deal is successfully closed, as per the Terms & Conditions agreed by the business, a transcript of the transactional information is given to ExMyB and the further due diligence is done upon that. ExMyB charges a success fee varying from 2.5%-3% basis amount of deal.
Is there any limitation on the industry of startup?
No, ExMyB has a pool of investors having different requirements to go ahead with their investments. If you and your business match the set of expectations, there is no cap or restriction on any type.
How do I seek investment for my business on ExMyB?
It is very easy to set up your profile that helps you seek out investment on ExMyB and get connected and network with potential investors. For the process to start, create a basic profile by filling out some questions required to make your profile. Once an account is created, write the BAT and our offline team gets in touch with you regarding the same. Also explained through diagrams in services section.
What if the startup fails?
The money invested by an investor is finally his/ her own decision and ExMyB hold no responsibility with respect to what the future of the company holds. Also, the terms and conditions between the founder and investor can be mutually decided upon and put across in the Share Holder Agreement.
Are Non residential Indians or and Foreigners permitted to invest in Indian companies?
YES. As per the Foreign Direct Investment Policy of India.
Is ExMyB SEBI compliant?
ExMyB does not act as a stock market in a ny capacity thus falls in line with any SEBI guideline.
